## Best practices

- **Don't spam**: send to one group per day max
- **Personalize**: add your own brief comment ("صار يفيدني كثيراً" / "I've been using it daily")
- **Don't over-pitch**: the app sells itself; you just open the door
- **Reply to questions personally**: don't auto-forward replies
- **Friday morning** = best send time (مفعّل لجمعة + استعداد للاستماع للخطبة)

## What NOT to do

- ❌ Forward as broadcast list (looks like spam)
- ❌ Send to groups you don't normally participate in
- ❌ Include too many emojis (looks unserious)
- ❌ Add "please share with everyone you know" (pressure tactic, harms the niyyah)
- ❌ Promise specific rewards ("100 hasanat for each share") — don't fabricate rewards
