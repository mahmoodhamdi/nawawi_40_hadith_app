# Khateeb Introduction Script — English

**Use case**: For imams to read after Jumu'ah prayer or during a study
circle in English-speaking communities. ~60-90 seconds.

---

## Short version (~60 seconds)

Assalamu alaikum wa rahmatullahi wa barakatuh.

My dear brothers and sisters, Imam an-Nawawi (may Allah have mercy on him)
collected forty hadiths that contain the foundations of Islam. Memorizing
and understanding them is among the greatest acts that benefit a Muslim.

I want to share with you a completely free app that contains these forty
hadiths with audio recitation by Sheikh Ahmad Al-Nafees, and a simple
explanation for each. The app:

- Works **fully offline** — you can use it anywhere
- Has **no advertising** at all
- Collects **no data** — your privacy is preserved
- Is **open source** — anyone can verify its integrity

This app is offered as **sadaqah jariyah** by a brother. Whoever shares it
and points others to it has the same reward as those who benefit from it.

Search your app store for: **"Forty Hadith Nawawi"**. It's light, simple,
and accessible to everyone.

May Allah benefit us with what we learn, and make us among those who
teach others good.

---

## Distribution tips

- Read after Jumu'ah, not during the khutbah
- Don't name the developer — this is sadaqah
- Don't mention prices — free is free
- Encourage youth — they benefit most
- If asked "who made it?" — say "one of our brothers, as ongoing charity"
