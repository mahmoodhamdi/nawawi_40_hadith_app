## Full description (max 4000 chars)

**Forty Hadith Nawawi — Narrated by Sheikh Ahmad Al-Nafees**
**Open-source ongoing charity — No ads, no tracking, no internet required**

A free Islamic app presenting the 42 Hadiths compiled by Imam an-Nawawi (may Allah have mercy on him), featuring clear audio recitation by Sheikh Ahmad Al-Nafees, and a simple explanation for each hadith.

### Why this app?

✓ **Fully offline** — All text and audio bundled, no internet needed
✓ **Zero ads** — No advertising, no affiliate links, ever
✓ **Zero tracking** — No analytics, no data collection, 100% private
✓ **Open source** — Code available on GitHub, contribute to the reward
✓ **Sadaqah Jariyah** — Every read, download, and share adds to your scale

### Features

📖 **Verified citations** for every hadith (narrator, source collection, sunnah.com link)
🔊 **Audio recitation** by Sheikh Ahmad Al-Nafees for all 42 hadiths
📚 **Simple explanation** for each hadith, drawn from major scholarly commentaries
🔍 **Smart search** by text or hadith number, with search history
⭐ **Favorites** to bookmark important hadiths
📊 **Reading progress** — resume from where you left off
🎨 **Four themes** (Light, Dark, Blue, Purple)
🌍 **Two languages**: Arabic and English with full RTL support
🔔 **Daily reminder** at the time you choose
🎯 **Focused reading mode** for reflection and memorization
🖼️ **Share hadiths as beautiful images** on WhatsApp/Twitter/Instagram
🔤 **Adjustable font size** separately for hadith and explanation

### Intention (Niyyah)

This app is offered as a continuing charity (sadaqah jariyah) to every Muslim. Our goal is to deliver the knowledge of Imam an-Nawawi (rahimahu Allah) to the largest possible number of Muslims worldwide. Join the reward by sharing this app with at least 3 friends.

### Contribute

The app is open-source under the MIT license. Whether you're a developer, translator, or designer — you can contribute on GitHub.

### Privacy

- No personal data collected
- No tracking SDKs
- Fully offline
- Only permission requested: Notifications (for the daily reminder)

May Allah make this an ongoing charity for everyone who contributed to it.
